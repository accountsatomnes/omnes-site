/// Design Principles

What guides the brand.

[01]
Institutional Precision
Every design decision should convey rigor and trustworthiness. Clean grids, controlled typography, generous whitespace. Nothing decorative that doesn't serve a function.
[02]
Warm Minimalism
The warm linen palette differentiates Omnes from cold institutional finance. Subtle grain texture and warm off-whites create a refined, approachable aesthetic without sacrificing authority.
[03]
Systematic Accents
Terra is used exclusively for primary CTAs and active states. Teal for positive/zero-fee indicators. Sand for warmth and tertiary information. Blue for data and informational contexts.
[04]
Section Label Convention
All section openers use the /// Label pattern — uppercase, 10.5px, 0.12em letter-spacing. This is a core visual motif across all Omnes communications and must be preserved.
