/// OMN, Series 1

The Omnes Mining Note.

A tokenized Bitcoin hashrate-backed debt security providing direct exposure to Bitcoin mining economics, institutional-grade, Luxembourg-issued, and fully on-chain verifiable.

  • Target raiseUS$50,000,000
  • Backing per OMN1 PH/s hashrate
  • Tenor36 months
  • Minimum investmentUS$100,000
  • Expense ratio3.75% total
  • Performance fee0%
  • DistributionBitcoin at maturity

The Omnes Mining Note is a tokenized hashrate-backed debt security issued under Luxembourg law, giving institutional investors direct exposure to Bitcoin mining economics without owning or operating infrastructure.

All mined Bitcoin accrues over the 36-month tenor and is distributed directly to investors at maturity, net of a flat 3.75% all-in expense ratio. No performance fee. No caps. No surprises.
